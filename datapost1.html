Building out a landing page in Tableau

Organizing Tableau dashboards using a centralized landing page

July 5, 2015 Data

My current project is organizing the many Tableau dashboards in my folders. Though not the most thrilling topic on face value, this project is important to my own personal workflow and mental health. I was clicking around looking for dashboards most days, usually inspired by someone mentioning in passing, "Oh yeah, I think we already made one for that metric two years ago," and I thought there may be a way to organize them by topic and navigate around using just one dashboard that I keep on hand. Initially, I hoped to hyperlink to all dashboards, but I then realized that would only be possible if all our dashboards were on the Tableau server. Surprise - they're not. Many of them have been stored in a shared drive, and I assume our Tableau server folks would not love the idea of bulk uploading all our dashboards just for fun when they ultimately don't need to be accessed by anyone but our little corner of the office. For now, I'm focusing on navigating to just a few topics that are coming up more frequently and can reasonably be kept in a Tableau file without crashing. I'll also likely include a hover option with the path to where the user can find dashboards about a content area. Not perfect, but better than tracking down someone who may know someone who can find where they saved that dashboard they made in the summer of 2020. More to come.
